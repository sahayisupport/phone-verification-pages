Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

Sahayi ("we", "us", "our") operates the Sahayi mobile application, a platform connecting customers, agents, and professionals across Kerala. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it's handled.

Information we collect

DataWhy we collect it
Phone numberTo verify your identity via one-time password (OTP) and enable login
OTP / verification codesTo confirm the phone number belongs to you at sign-up and login
Name & profile detailsTo show other users who they're matched with for a task
Location (approximate or precise, with permission)To match you with nearby agents, professionals, or job posts
Task details & messagesTo coordinate jobs between customers and agents/professionals
Payment detailsTo process payments for completed tasks (handled by our payment processor, not stored by us directly)
ID / skill verification documentsTo verify agents and professionals before they can accept jobs
Device & usage dataTo keep the app secure and improve performance

How OTP verification works

When you sign up or log in, we send a one-time password to your phone number using Firebase Authentication (a Google service). We do not read or store the contents of your SMS inbox — only the verification code exchange needed to confirm the OTP is required to complete sign-in.

How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account
  • To match customers with agents or professionals nearby
  • To facilitate communication and payment between users for a posted task
  • To verify the identity and credentials of agents and professionals
  • To respond to support requests and resolve disputes between users
  • To improve app reliability and prevent fraud or abuse

Who we share it with

We share data only where necessary to run the app:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud — authentication, hosting, and backend infrastructure
  • Payment processors — to complete in-app payments
  • Other users — limited profile and task details needed to complete a job (e.g. name, rating, approximate location)

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

Data retention

We retain account and task data for as long as your account is active, and for a limited period afterward to meet legal, accounting, or dispute-resolution needs. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your choices

  • You can edit or delete your profile information from within the app.
  • You can withdraw location permission at any time through your device settings, though this may limit matching.
  • You can request a copy of your data or full account deletion by contacting us.

Children's privacy

Sahayi is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Sahayi's features evolve. Material changes will be notified in-app or via the contact email below.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to sahayi.support@gmail.com.
